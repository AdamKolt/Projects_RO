B2B Sales Academy

Tiered Certification Blended Learning AI Generated Content RAG Knowledge Assistant

Built and ran the B2B Sales Academy at Ooredoo Qatar — a two-tier certification program serving 200+ frontline sellers, account managers, and channel partners across Mobile, Fixed, and ICT product lines.

Fragmented onboarding and inconsistent product knowledge were costing the SMB commercial organization negative customer experience, slow ramp time, and lower conversion rate. The B2B Sales Academy replaced that with a structured, tiered-certification program anchored to Ooredoo’s sales stages — and a modern AI-native enablement stack based on CoPilot and Claude that augmented collateral with content on lead discovery, objections handling, use cases, cheat sheets, and competitive analysis.

Tiered Certification Architecture: Designed a two-tier certification ladder — Sales Associate (foundational coverage of products, tooling, and core sales motions) and Sales Expert (complex selling, solution design, and vertical selling). Each tier anchored to Ooredoo's core product platforms (Fixed, Mobile, and ICT) so progression mapped directly to the pipeline reality reps navigated daily. Certifications gated access to complex deals and channel partner tiers.
Differentiated Sales Methodologies: Embedded deal-size-appropriate sales methodologies into the curriculum. Adopted MEDDPICC for complex enterprise and government bids, Challenger Sale for mid-market opportunities, and Command of the Message for SMB volume sales. Each methodology was operationalized through CMS-housed playbooks, role-plays, certification assessments, and reinforcement in the manager coaching cadence.
Sales Plays Activation: The Academy was the engine that activated all concurrent Sales Plays across the SMB portfolio — product launches, VAS cross-sells (M365 attach doubled seats two years), industry plays, segment plays, and channel plays for the SOHO program. CMS assets — battlecards, talk tracks, discovery banks, certification quizzes, and playbooks — flowed directly into Play execution, with quarterly review and refresh.
Curriculum Across Three Product Lines: Built use-case-first curriculum covering all three product lines (Mobile, Fixed, ICT) plus core tooling (SFA, CRM, CMS, CPQ). Modules blended product depth, discovery & qualification, objection handling, pricing and CPQ, and soft skills / executive presence — sequenced so each module built on the prior, culminating in a tier certification assessment.
Role-Targeted Tracks & LMS Governance: Not every session fit every team. Given the diversity of audiences — direct reps, account executives, sector specialists, pre-sales, telemarketing, retention, order management, retail/shops, and chat support — the curriculum was segmented into role-specific tracks, with each session targeted at the teams whose motion it supported. The LMS drove assignment, tracked individual completion, surfaced manager-level gaps, and auto-issued tier certifications upon assessment passage. At the end of every session, a QR code on screen routed attendees directly into the post-training quiz — capturing assessment scores at the moment of peak retention and feeding them straight into the LMS for tracking.

Role-targeted tracks — per-attendee completion and gaps.

AI-Native Content Factory: Pioneered an AI-generated enablement content pipeline using GPT-4o and Claude to produce discovery question banks by vertical and persona, objection-and-response libraries for common and nuanced pushback, industry use-case cheat sheets mapped to product bundles, competitive teardowns and positioning guides, and MCQ certification quizzes auto-graded in the LMS. Content cycles compressed from weeks to days, keeping pace with a telecom product catalog that changed almost weekly.
RAG-Based Knowledge Assistant In-Workflow: Deployed a retrieval-augmented generative assistant grounded on the live tariff library, CRM context, product catalog, objection libraries, and certification curriculum. Reps and partner agents queried the assistant directly in-workflow — discovery prompts, competitor positioning, bundle fit, and pricing guardrails — and received answers cited back to source. The assistant turned the academy's content into a continuously-available coach, closing the gap between classroom certification and live deal execution.
Blended Delivery — ILT + Live Broadcast + Recorded + CMS: Orchestrated a bench of 15 product-manager instructors plus specialized SMEs, delivering weekly instructor-led cohorts reinforced by periodic boot camps and weekly performance reviews.

Every ILT session was simultaneously broadcast over Teams for field reps who couldn't attend in person, and recorded for publication to the enablement CMS for on-demand consumption. For shop and chat-support agents who couldn't attend live, work schedules were designed so they completed the relevant recorded session before starting their shift — ensuring every team member received the same training without losing instructor-led rigor.
Partner Academy Extension: Extended the academy to serve the SOHO indirect channel partners program — 5 partner firms with ~125 agents. Partner agents completed the same certification path as internal sellers, enabling a consistent sales-and-retention motion across direct, indirect, retail, and telemarketing channels. This was the foundation that let partners sell and retain customers independently without degrading experience.

Outcomes

  • 85% certification completion rate across the 200+ cohort.
  • 15% post-certification performance uplift measured against pre-cert attainment.
  • 50% reduction in frontline ramp time (4 months → 2 months).
  • Reps quality of knowledge reflected positively in CSAT and NPS across the SMB commercial organization.
  • Consistent on-message sales-and-retention motions across direct and indirect channels, enabling the SOHO partner program to deliver both sales and retention independently.
