Lead-to-Cash Optimization

Telecom SMB Sector Revenue Operations Pipeline Acceleration Sales & CX Enablement

Ooredoo Qatar's SMB sales operation spans 100+ indirect channel partner agents, multiple retail outlets, a direct field sales force, and an outbound call center — each operating as a largely independent motion with limited data sharing, inconsistent tooling, and fragmented order workflows.

The Lead-to-Cash pipeline had multiple friction points suppressing revenue and inflating costs: leads were identified reactively, agents lacked the data and tools to engage effectively, orders moved through manual paper-based processes, and leadership had no reliable visibility into performance. I led five concurrent initiatives to systematically eliminate each bottleneck — from first customer contact through to cash collection and executive reporting.

1

Data-Driven Outbound Engine

The call center was operating reactively — agents had no targeted prospect lists, no structured scripts, and no mechanism to hand off warm leads to field teams. Outreach was cold, conversion was low, and qualified opportunities were routinely lost.

Mined the customer database to identify high-propensity segments based on usage patterns, tenure, and product gaps. Generated targeted outbound calling lists enriched with account context — giving each agent a data-backed story tailored specifically to that customer before the call was placed.

Calling lists and accompanying scripts were uploaded directly into the centralized calling module, giving agents a single, structured workflow for every outreach. When a call converted, agents could close the sale on the spot or transfer the qualified lead to a field agent in real time — ensuring no warm lead was lost in handoff. This closed-loop engine, from data insight to call to close, eliminated cold calling, improved conversion rates, and created a measurable, repeatable outbound revenue motion.

2

Unified SFA & Field Enablement

Field reps had no mobile access to the CRM or SFA platform, and sales collateral was scattered across personal folders and shared drives — leaving teams without the information or tools needed to engage customers effectively in the field.

Enabled and customized the mobile module of the company's Oracle SFA platform, giving the field team full lead management capability on the go. Integrated it with the CRM to surface customer contacts, active services, and account history — enabling informed conversations and on-the-spot appointment scheduling. Extended the module to allow reps to capture market intelligence in real time, including competitor services, new contacts, and updated account details.

Complementing this, deployed a centralized Content Management platform as the single source of truth for all sales collateral — presentations, product specs, pricing, promotions, and order forms. Equipped every field representative with a connected tablet for real-time access to collateral, customer data, and order processing — removing the last excuses for showing up unprepared.

3

Order Digitization & Cycle Time Reduction

Non-standardized, incomplete paper order forms were causing downstream operational errors, fulfillment delays, and a poor customer experience — with the order signing cycle routinely stretching to 48 hours.

Partnered with Product, Operations, and Marketing UX/UI teams to redesign and standardize all order forms into clean, fillable PDF documents. Centralized them in the sales repository for online completion, download, or direct sharing with customers. Established a digital signing workflow enabling authorized customer signatories to execute orders remotely — compressing the signing step from 48 hours to under one hour. This was the critical unlock in a broader process redesign that cut the total order-to-activation cycle from 9 days to 3.

4

Customer Engagement & In-Store Experience

Sales staff lacked effective tools to engage walk-in customers in retail locations, resulting in missed upsell opportunities and a flat, undifferentiated in-store experience.

Designed an interactive product and services catalog for both shop and field environments — deployed on interactive screens across all retail locations for self-serve browsing, and equipped field reps with the same catalog for guided, consultative conversations. Integrated promotional messaging into the queue management system, turning passive wait time into an active upsell trigger.

The initiative drove a measurable lift in cross-sell and upsell conversion, contributing to a +12% improvement in revenue per account across the SMB base. Combined with faster, more informed service interactions across all channels, CSAT improved from 67% to 84% — the highest score the SMB division had recorded.

5

Revenue Intelligence & Executive Reporting

Fragmented data sources and manual reporting created blind spots in pipeline performance, limited accountability across the sales force, and left leadership without a reliable, timely view of the business.

Engineered automated data pipelines with CRM and Billing developers, consolidating sales performance data by agent, zone, and product alongside churn and number portability metrics. Built an ETL layer using SQL and Power Query, then developed an interactive reporting suite in Excel and Power BI to surface trends and performance stories clearly.

Designed a monthly Revenue Intelligence Pack for C-suite consumption, consolidating KPIs across revenue, churn, NPS, workforce utilization, and pipeline into a single, visually consistent deck. Eliminated 40 hours per month of manual report preparation and established a single source of truth for strategic decision-making across the entire SMB commercial operation.

Together, these five initiatives transformed a fragmented, reactive sales operation into a structured, data-driven revenue engine — shortening the order cycle from 9 days to 3, lifting revenue per account by 12%, and improving NPS by 18 points. Every intervention targeted a specific L2C bottleneck, and the cumulative effect was a pipeline that was faster, more predictable, and measurably more profitable.
