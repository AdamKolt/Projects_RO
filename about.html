About Me

I build the teams, operations, and systems that
make revenue predictable.

I am a Commercial Operations & Revenue Enablement leader who turns GTM strategy into predictable, scalable growth by empowering sellers, building channels and partners, and engineering the playbooks, dashboards, and AI-native systems that make it stick. My career spans Telecom, State & Local Government, and IT, always at the intersection where strategy meets execution.

The results speak directly to the mandate: scaling a startup from $5.8M to $57.8M in four years by codifying a national sales motion and rollout blueprint across 13 states; building a B2B Sales Academy that certified 200+ sellers and channel partners and cut frontline ramp from 4 to 2 months; sustaining 5% YoY growth and driving NRR to 104% across a $200M SMB P&L; deploying an AI-native enablement layer (CoPilot, Claude, RAG on GPT-4o) that compressed time-to-quote; and orchestrating hyperscaler and OEM partnerships whose VAS attach motion doubled M365 seats two years running and lifted ARPA 5%.

I work across the full commercial stack — academies and certification programs, channel and partner development, a PLM-embedded content operations system as the single source of truth, CRM architecture, pipeline analytics, and Lead-to-Cash optimization — orchestrating Sales Plays with a quarterly review and refresh cadence and equipping sellers with MEDDPICC, Challenger, and Command of the Message fluency. I’ve managed a multi-vendor managed-services stack (Cisco Meraki, Huawei Smart Wi-Fi, NGFW, CCTV-as-a-Service, connected POS) and actively embed AI and automation into GTM workflows to eliminate manual effort and reduce pipeline friction.

What makes this possible is a combination of commercial and technical fluency uncommon in commercial leadership: an MBA, a B.E.E., and formal project management credentials — enough to move from boardroom strategy to system design in the same conversation, and to translate cleanly between business stakeholders and engineering teams.

I am looking to bring this experience to a Commercial Operations, Sales Enablement, or RevOps role where the mandate is growth and the expectation is building the infrastructure to support it.
