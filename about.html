About Me


I build the systems that make revenue predictable.

My work sits at the intersection of Revenue Operations, digital transformation, and product innovation — where strategy meets execution and process meets technology. I built my career helping organizations in Telecom, Government, and IT fix the gaps between GTM ambitions and operational reality.

From designing Lead-to-Cash lifecycles and eliminating friction across Marketing, Sales, and CX handoffs, to building the data infrastructure that gives leadership actionable insights, the results have been tangible: scaling a startup’s revenue 10× in four years, cutting Customer Acquisition Cost by 20% through digital-first strategy, and generating $400K in new revenue within 90 days of launching a first-to-market data monetization product.

I work across the full revenue stack — CRM architecture, pipeline analytics, process automation, and product-led growth — using tools like Value Stream Mapping, unit economics, and Agile delivery to accelerate decisions and compress time-to-value. More recently, I’ve been embedding AI and automation into GTM workflows, including a RAG-based chatbot that automated B2B lead qualification and reduced digital order CAC by 50%.

What makes me effective is a unique blend of commercial and technical fluency: an MBA, a B.E.E., and formal project management credentials — allowing me to move from boardroom strategy to deep system design in the same conversation, and translate between business stakeholders and engineering teams without losing either audience.
