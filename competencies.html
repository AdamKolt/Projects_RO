1 Revenue Strategy & Commercial Operations Strategy

  • Leads 100+ person multi-function commercial organizations — account management, indirect channels, SDR/telemarketing, retention, retail, and support — structured for segment coverage and aligned to the revenue motion.
  • Designs revenue models and GTM business cases grounded in unit economics — LTV/CAC ratios, payback periods, and margin contribution.
  • Builds data-driven revenue forecasting and pipeline health frameworks — giving leadership real-time visibility into funnel performance, conversion rates, and revenue risk.
  • Designs Go-to-Market launch frameworks that compress time-to-revenue — from co-branded partner propositions and pricing strategies to structured sales enablement programs.
  • Builds territory design, quota-setting, and capacity models — aligning rep headcount to bookings targets, balancing coverage equitably, and sizing quota from pipeline potential rather than prior-year math.
  • Designs sales compensation plans and incentive structures — plan accelerators, SPIFFs, and On-Target Earnings (OTE) modeling that steer rep behavior toward strategic motions (new logo, expansion, multi-year).
  • Defines Ideal Customer Profile (ICP), segmentation, and account tiering — translating firmographic and intent signals into named-account lists, tiered coverage models, and differentiated GTM plays per segment.

2 GTM Systems & Revenue Architecture Execution

  • Architects and optimizes CRM platforms and revenue tech stacks — designing data models, lead routing logic, pipeline stages, and automation workflows.
  • Builds sales enablement infrastructure — from CMS-driven sales process to CPQ configuration — reducing agent ramp-up from 4 to 2 months and improving conversion consistency across channels.
  • Implements AI-powered GTM tools — including a RAG-based sales chatbot integrating GPT-4o with CRM and product data, cutting digital orders CAC by 50%.
  • Stands up deal desk and pricing governance — discount approval matrices, non-standard term reviews, and margin guardrails that accelerate complex deals without eroding unit economics.
  • Architects partner and channel operations — deal registration, co-sell workflows, partner portals, and attribution logic that scale indirect revenue without double-paying commissions.

3 Lead-to-Cash Optimization Execution

  • Redesigns end-to-end Lead-to-Cash processes to eliminate revenue leakage and handoff friction — cutting order-to-activation from 9 days to 3 days.
  • Applies Value Stream Mapping and BPMN to model As-Is / To-Be states across Marketing, Sales, and CX — surfacing high-impact automation opportunities and eliminating non-value-adding steps.
  • Engineers hybrid fulfillment architectures blending self-serve automation with assisted channels — auto-provisioning core products while preserving human touchpoints for complex deals.
  • Engineers renewal and expansion motions — health scoring, auto-renew vs. managed-renewal segmentation, and cross-sell playbooks that lift NRR without scaling CS headcount linearly.
  • Drives SaaS and cloud attach across the SMB base — seat-based pricing, renewal playbooks, and co-sell motions with hyperscalers (Microsoft 365, Google Workspace, Copilot, NotebookLM, IaaS, Defender, DDoS) that doubled M365 seats two years running and strengthened NRR through product-mix expansion.
  • Operationalizes commission calculation and payout workflows — integrating CRM, CPQ, and comp engines to eliminate payout disputes and close monthly commission cycles faster.

4 Data, Analytics & Revenue Intelligence Data & AI

  • Architects omnichannel data capture frameworks that synchronize CRM, product, and behavioral touchpoints into a single source of truth — uplifting outbound conversion rate from 18 to 25% and equipping field sales with real-time intelligence.
  • Builds predictive churn and retention models analyzing bill shock, usage drop-off, and support escalations — improving retention save-ratio by 18% and preventing churn before it materializes.
  • Defines and operationalizes RevOps KPIs — pipeline coverage, win rates, CAC, LTV, NRR, and time-to-close — translating raw performance data into actionable insights for sales leadership and executive stakeholders.
  • Instills CRM data quality and governance — field standards, duplicate/merge logic, enrichment workflows, and activity-capture discipline that keep forecasts and attribution trustworthy.
  • Runs structured win/loss analysis programs — competitor displacement patterns, deal-stage drop-off root causes, and pricing sensitivity signals that feed back into product, pricing, and enablement.

  • Orchestrates Marketing, Sales, and CX alignment around shared revenue goals — establishing common definitions (MQL, SQL, opportunity stages), unified handoff protocols, and joint accountability frameworks.
  • Drives executive alignment on revenue strategy through structured governance (RACI), scenario-based roadmaps, and board-level presentations — translating operational complexity into clear investment decisions.
  • Builds and coaches high-performing RevOps teams — embedding a culture of data-driven decision-making, requirements rigor, and continuous process improvement across commercial functions.
  • Owns the annual operating plan cadence — cascading board-approved bookings targets into segment quotas, capacity plans, and hiring ramps, with quarterly re-plans when macro or pipeline signals shift.
