The competencies below ladder up to these three layers.

1 Revenue Strategy & Commercial Operations

  • Designs revenue models and GTM business cases grounded in unit economics — LTV/CAC ratios, payback periods, and margin contribution.
  • Builds data-driven revenue forecasting and pipeline health frameworks — giving leadership real-time visibility into funnel performance, conversion rates, and revenue risk.
  • Designs Go-to-Market launch frameworks that compress time-to-revenue — from co-branded partner propositions and pricing strategies to structured sales enablement programs.

2 GTM Systems & Revenue Architecture

  • Architects and optimizes CRM platforms and revenue tech stacks — designing data models, lead routing logic, pipeline stages, and automation workflows.
  • Builds sales enablement infrastructure — from CMS-driven sales process to CPQ configuration — reducing agent ramp-up from 4 to 2 months and improving conversion consistency across channels.
  • Implements AI-powered GTM tools — including a RAG-based sales chatbot integrating GPT-4o with CRM and product data, cutting digital orders CAC by 50%.

3 Lead-to-Cash Optimization

  • Redesigns end-to-end Lead-to-Cash processes to eliminate revenue leakage and handoff friction — cutting order-to-activation from 9 days to 3 days.
  • Applies Value Stream Mapping, BPMN, and Lean to model As-Is / To-Be states across Marketing, Sales, and CX — surfacing high-impact automation opportunities and eliminating non-value-adding steps.
  • Engineers hybrid fulfillment architectures blending self-serve automation with assisted channels — auto-provisioning core products while preserving human touchpoints for complex deals.

4 Data, Analytics & Revenue Intelligence

  • Architects omnichannel data capture frameworks that synchronize CRM, product, and behavioral touchpoints into a single source of truth — uplifting outbound conversion rate from 18 to 25% and equipping field sales with real-time intelligence.
  • Builds predictive churn and retention models analyzing bill shock, usage drop-off, and support escalations — improving retention save-ratio by 18% and preventing churn before it materializes.
  • Defines and operationalizes RevOps KPIs — pipeline coverage, win rates, CAC, LTV, NRR, and time-to-close — translating raw performance data into actionable insights for sales leadership and executive stakeholders.

5 GTM Alignment & Cross-Functional Leadership

  • Orchestrates Marketing, Sales, and CX alignment around shared revenue goals — establishing common definitions (MQL, SQL, opportunity stages), unified handoff protocols, and joint accountability frameworks.
  • Drives executive alignment on revenue strategy through structured governance (RACI), scenario-based roadmaps, and board-level presentations — translating operational complexity into clear investment decisions.
  • Builds and coaches high-performing RevOps teams — embedding a culture of data-driven decision-making, requirements rigor, and continuous process improvement across commercial functions.
